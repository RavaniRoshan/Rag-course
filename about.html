This comprehensive course is designed for AI Engineers and Data Scientists who want to master RAG systems from first principles to production deployment.

info What is RAG?

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI framework that combines the power of large language models with external knowledge retrieval. Instead of relying solely on the model's training data, RAG systems retrieve relevant information from external sources and use it to generate more accurate, up-to-date, and contextually relevant responses.
